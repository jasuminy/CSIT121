My Favorite Stuffed Delicious Chicken Breast Recipe

Gimme Delicious

Nutrition Facts

Serving size: 3 Chicken Breast
Calories 400

Cholesterol 50.3 mg

Ingredients List

  • 3 thin slices of chicken breast
  • Steam broccoli florets
  • Red peppers or yellow peppers
  • Shedded cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Cream cheese
  • Paprika powder
  • Olive oil
  • Toothpicks
  • Knife
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Large Pan
  • Baking sheet

    • Instructions

  • First, If the chicken is frozen then make sure it's completely thawed or leave until completely thawed before cleaning.
  • Next, Remove any excess skin from the chicken.
  • Then, Rinse the chicken inside out with white vinegar and Rub with lemon wedges and sprinkle with coarse salt and pepper.
  • Lastly, Rinse with white vinegar.
  • For the filling you will need to steam broccoli florets in boiling water for 5 mins and after they're cooked run them under cold water, drain and chop them into small chunks and cut the red or yellow bell peppers that you would like to use into small pieces
  • Season the chicken breasts front and back with paprika powder, garlic powder, and italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  • Use a sharp knife to slice each chicken breasts horizontally to form a pocket
  • Grab a large mixing bowl and put the small chunks of broccoli and small pieces of bell peppers also add the cream cheese, salt and peopper, and shedded cheese into the bowl and mix everything up and stuff into butterflied chicken breasts use the toothpick to close the chicken with the filling inside.
  • Preheat the oven to 400F.
  • Pour some Olive oil into a large pan (which pan you want to use) over medium high heat.
  • Add the chicken breast to the medium high heat pan for 2 or 3 minutes per side
  • After All the chicken breasts are done, cover the baking sheet with the aluminum foil place them on top then into the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes until cooked through.
